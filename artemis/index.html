About Artemis Hospitals

Artemis Health Institute was established in 2007 and is a healthcare venture launched by the Apollo Tyres Group promoters. Artemis was the first hospital in Gurgaon to get Joint Commission International (J.C.I.) accreditation in 2013 and also the first Hospital in Haryana to get NABH accreditation within 3 years of start-up It is designed as one of the exceptional hospitals in India, providing expertise in advanced medical & surgical interventions in inpatient and outpatient services. Artemis has provided modern technology in the hands of reputed doctors from all over the country and overseas to set a new benchmark in healthcare. The medical practices and procedures that the hospital adhered to are standard among the finest in the world. Outstanding services, a warm, open patient-centric environment, and affordability have made Artemis among the most respected hospitals in the country. It has 400 full-time doctors, 12 centers of excellence, and 40 specialties.

FOUNDER ARTEMIS Hospital, located in Gurgaon, Delhi NCR, India, was founded by Dr. Devi Shetty, who is a renowned cardiac surgeon and the founder of Narayana Health, one of India's largest healthcare conglomerates. ARTEMIS Hospital was established with the vision of providing world-class medical care and facilities to patients in India and around the world. It offers a wide range of medical services and specialties, including cardiology, oncology, orthopedics, neurology, and more. The hospital is known for its state-of-the-art infrastructure, advanced medical technology, and a team of highly skilled doctors and healthcare professionals. ARTEMIS Hospital in Delhi has an International Patient Care Unit (IPCU) dedicated to catering to the needs of patients coming from overseas. This unit is specifically designed to ensure that international patients receive personalized care and assistance throughout their medical journey at ARTEMIS.
