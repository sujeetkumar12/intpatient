About Aster Medcity Hospitals

Aster Medcity is equipped with 670 beds, a quaternary care facility in a soothingly beautiful 40-acre waterfront campus & is among the best hospitals in Kochi, Kerala. It is the first JCI-accredited quaternary care multispecialty hospital in the state and is a blend of talent and technology to provide comprehensive treatment with a multidisciplinary approach. The center has been providing quality healthcare at a reasonable cost through its Centres of Excellence and a multispecialty hospital since 2013. Kerala’s first quaternary care hospital, Aster Medcity, has also been accredited by NABH and received NABH certification for Nursing Excellence and Green OT Certification by Bureau Veritas, all in the first year of opening its door to the world.
