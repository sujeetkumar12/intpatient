About Us

Fortis Healthcare

Fortis Healthcare Limited – is an IHH Healthcare Berhad Company, a prime integrated healthcare services provider in India. It is amongst one of the country’s largest healthcare organizations, with approximately 40 healthcare facilities, developing projects, 4000 operational beds, and more than 400 diagnostics centers. It draws support from its partnership with the parent company, IHH, to build its culture of exceptional patient care and magnificent clinical excellence. Fortis employs over 23,000 people with the same vision of becoming the world’s most trusted healthcare network. Fortis offers a total extent of comprehensive healthcare services ranging from clinics to quaternary care facilities and a broad range of ancillary services.
